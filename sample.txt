Natural language processing is a field of artificial intelligence.
It focuses on enabling computers to understand and generate human language.
Text summarization is one of its key applications.
Extractive summarization selects existing sentences that carry important information.
TF-IDF is a common weighting scheme that highlights informative terms.
By scoring each sentence using TF-IDF, we can select a concise and useful summary.
This approach is simple, interpretable, and effective for many practical tasks.
